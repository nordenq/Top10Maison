Our evaluation framework focuses on practical factors that affect everyday use. A 5–6 qt air fryer suits most homes, single baskets stay simpler and cheaper, and airflow power matters more than extra presets.

<section class="space-y-4">
  <h2 class="text-2xl font-semibold text-ink">Pick the right size</h2>
  <p class="text-sm text-ink/70">Match capacity to your household so you’re not cooking in batches.</p>
  <div class="grid gap-4 md:grid-cols-3">
    <div class="card p-5">
      <p class="text-sm font-semibold text-ink">1–2 people</p>
      <p class="mt-2 text-lg font-semibold text-ink">2–4 qt</p>
      <p class="mt-2 text-sm text-ink/70">Faster cooking, takes less space.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="card p-5">
      <p class="text-sm font-semibold text-ink">3–4 people</p>
      <p class="mt-2 text-lg font-semibold text-ink">5–6 qt</p>
      <p class="mt-2 text-sm text-ink/70">Best balance for most homes.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="card p-5">
      <p class="text-sm font-semibold text-ink">5+ people</p>
      <p class="mt-2 text-lg font-semibold text-ink">7+ qt or dual basket</p>
      <p class="mt-2 text-sm text-ink/70">Fewer batches, easier full meals.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="space-y-4 mt-10">
  <h2 class="text-2xl font-semibold text-ink">Buying considerations</h2>
  <div class="grid gap-6 lg:grid-cols-2">
    <div class="card p-5">
      <h3 class="text-sm font-semibold text-ink">Single basket vs dual basket</h3>
      <ul class="mt-3 list-clean space-y-2 text-sm text-ink/70">
        <li>Single basket: cheaper, simpler, best for beginners.</li>
        <li>Dual basket: cook two foods at once, better for families, costs more and takes more space.</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="card p-5">
      <h3 class="text-sm font-semibold text-ink">Price vs value</h3>
      <ul class="mt-3 list-clean space-y-2 text-sm text-ink/70">
        <li>$60–$120: basic controls, less consistent airflow.</li>
        <li>$120–$200: best value with even cooking and a solid build.</li>
        <li>$200–$300: dual baskets and convenience features.</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="space-y-4 mt-10">
  <h2 class="text-2xl font-semibold text-ink">Features that actually matter</h2>
  <div class="grid gap-4 lg:grid-cols-2">
    <div class="card p-5 bg-surface">
      <h3 class="text-sm font-semibold text-ink">Features that matter</h3>
      <ul class="mt-3 list-clean space-y-2 text-sm text-ink/70">
        <li>Capacity — determines how often you cook in batches</li>
        <li>Airflow power — affects crispiness and speed</li>
        <li>Basket coating — makes cleaning easier and helps it last longer</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="card p-5 bg-surface">
      <h3 class="text-sm font-semibold text-ink">Features you can ignore</h3>
      <ul class="mt-3 list-clean space-y-2 text-sm text-ink/70">
        <li>Dozens of presets</li>
        <li>App connectivity</li>
        <li>Fancy touchscreens</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>
